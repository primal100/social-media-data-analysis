1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Permanent Motion App ("we," "our," or "the App") which collects and processes data through the TikTok API. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data We Collect
When you authorize the App via TikTok, we access the following information:

Public Profile Info: Your TikTok username and display name.

Video Metadata: Captions, hashtags, view counts, likes, and share counts of your last 20 videos.

Technical Data: Video duration and creation timestamps.

3. Purpose of Processing
We process this data for the following "Legitimate Interests" (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR):

To provide you with an AI-ready structured dataset of your TikTok performance.

To analyze content trends and engagement for personal brand strategy.

Future Use: If we expand this tool for others, data will be processed solely to provide requested analytics to that specific user.

4. Data Retention and Storage
Personal Use: Data is downloaded locally to your environment or a secure VPS.

Retention: We do not store your data longer than necessary to generate your reports. You can delete the data at any time by deleting the generated JSON files.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)
Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

Access: Request a copy of the data we have collected.

Erasure: Request that we delete your data.

Withdraw Consent: You can revoke the App's access at any time through your TikTok Settings > Security & Login > Manage App Access.
